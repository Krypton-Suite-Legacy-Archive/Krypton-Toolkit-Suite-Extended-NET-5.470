Remarks
Items may contain additional bounds because of several graphical occourences, mostly when ExtendedControls.ExtendedToolkit.Calendar in [!:CalendarDaysMode.Short] mode, due to the duration of the item; e.g. when an all day item lasts several weeks, one rectangle for week must be drawn to indicate the presence of the item.
